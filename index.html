HI I'M LISETTE

I am a creative explorer with an Engineering degree in Computer Science, though I often find that my most profound inspiration strikes far away from a computer screen. My life is a constant, vibrant pursuit of balance between logic and art—a mix of rhythms and textures where I’m either playing guitar, singing, or finding my focus through the steady pace of knitting and fitness. I have a deep-seated love for visual storytelling and content creation; I spend a significant part of my days hunting for that one unique visual or the perfect track that makes a project truly resonate. For me, everything is about the chase for that creative spark and the pure joy of making something new, whether it's through a camera lens, a melody, or a complex line of code.
